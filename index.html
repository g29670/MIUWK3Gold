    ABOUT the Application

    This application will allow you to store your current collection of movies in a central data location. You will be able to document your DVD, Blu-ray, and ,for the diehard movie fans, VHS videos. You will find that this app will help you or your guest choose a movie quickly for whatever mood you are in.
